Work Experience

Computer Science Tutor

I currently work for the Computer Science department at Hendrix as a tutor for lower-level Computer Science courses. My time here is generally spent helping students in these courses understand the fundamentals of what they’re doing and how they can be applied in a more general way.

Acxiom Corporation

From January to August of 2015, I worked for Acxiom Corporation as a Software Development Intern. This was an invaluable experience as it really taught me what it was like to develop in a corporate environment. I did a number of things during my time there, including:

  • Planning the project that I was brought on to assist with. We settled on a system design and moved on to implementation from here.
  • Developed several Python classes and utilities that were used throughout the project.
  • Furthered my experience with version-control systems and learned what it’s like when using one in a team. In particular, our project used Git.
  • Packaged multiple release cycles of our project using Redhat Package Manager for deployment on Acxiom’s infrastructure.
  • With HTML/CSS and JavaScript/jQuery, I created a user-friendly web interface for our project. This also involved a Flask back-end and our REST API.

MRE Consulting

For the summer of 2014 (May - August) I worked as an intern for the Information Technology help-desk at MRE Consulting.
